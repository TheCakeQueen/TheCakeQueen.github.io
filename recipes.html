Brownies

Best fudgiest brownies
Ingredients
240g butter
Vegetable/olive oil 30ml
260g sugar
4 Eggs
15ml Vanilla
Salt tsp
130g Flour
100g Cocoa powder
200g Chocolate chips

    1. Say Bismillah
    2. Preheat oven and grease pan
    3. Combine butter, oil, sugar and whisk
    4. Add eggs, vanilla and beat until lighter in colour then add salt and flour
    5. Sift flour and cocoa powder and salt in new bowl
    6. Gently fold in wet ingredients and chocolate
    7. Pour in pan and bake for 25-30 minutes
    8. EAT

Bread Rolls


Ingredients
500g strong white bread flour , plus extra for dusting

7g sachet fast action yeast
1 tsp white caster sugar
2 tsp fine salt
1 tsp sunflower oil , plus extra for the work surface and bowl

    1. Say Bismillah
    2. Tip the flour, yeast, sugar, salt and oil into a bowl. Pour over 325ml warm water, then mix (with a spatula or your hand), until it comes together as a shaggy dough. Make sure all the flour has been incorporated. Cover and leave for 10 mins.
    3. Lightly oil your work surface and tip the dough onto it. Knead the dough for at least 10 mins until it becomes tighter and springy - if you have a stand mixer you can do this with a dough hook for 5 mins. Pull the dough into a ball and put in a clean, oiled bowl. Leave for 1 hr, or until doubled in size.
    4. Tip the dough onto a lightly floured surface and roll into a long sausage shape. Halve the dough, then divide each half into four pieces, so you have eight equal-sized portions. Roll each into a tight ball and put on a dusted baking tray, leaving some room between each ball for rising. Cover with a damp tea towel and leave in a warm place to prove for 40 mins-1 hr or until almost doubled in size.
    5. Heat the oven to 230C/210C fan/gas 8. When the dough is ready, dust each ball with a bit more flour. (If you like, you can glaze the rolls with milk or beaten egg, and top with seeds.) Bake for 25-30mins, until light brown and hollow sounding when tapped on the base. Leave to cool on a wire rack.
    6. EAT
    7. This recipe is from BBC GOODFOOD https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/recipes/easy-bread-rolls

Cornish Pasties


Ingredients
500g of plain flour [I use BeRo plain flour] , plus extra for dusting
200g of butter
4-6 tsp of water
A pinch of salt
However much cubed or minced meat you want or whatever filling you like
Swede
potatoes
2 medeim carrots
1 onion
1 egg

    1. Mix the butter and flour until like breadcrumbs
    2. Then add water and salt and chill for 20 mins
    3. While chilling make the filling and cook filling
    4. When both are done roll out the pastry however thick you like and add filling
    5. Pinch ends with your finger and then use a fork to crimp
    6. Cook until golden brown
    7. Egg wash and then put in oven until golden brown
